Example Data Vignette

#Example data This data comes from our wastewater and case data merged together by site and date. Then filtered with by 3 sites; Green Bay, Janesville, and Mauston. Then a new column is added; geo_mean which is the geometric mean of N1 and N2. This gives roughly 500 rows to work with for examples across different sized sites over a large time frame.

library(DSIWastewater)
